Health & Education

Prathima Institute of Medical Sciences established in 2001 under Prathima Educational Society registered under Societies Act. At Karimnagar, Hyderabad with the mission to bring health care of established standards within the reach of every individual; to educate the masses of the health and family welfare; commitment to excellence in research, healthcare and medical education. The people of 9 surrounding district will be immensely benefited.

The college is recognized by Govt. of India and Medical Council of India and is affiliated by Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Vijayawada. The medical college commenced with 150 MBBS admissions annually and started P.G. Medical and Diploma courses.

At PRATIMA, we have an illustrious track record of delivering unparalleled medical education and healthcare services since 15 years Prathima Hospitals ranked as the best hospital in Hyderabad. As part of our blueprint for future expansion, we are escalating our bed strength to 1500 by introducing our cutting -edge and contemporary multi-specialty multi-speciality hospitals in the prime centers of Hyderabad at Kachiguda and Kukatpally. True to our legacy of quality, the hospitals feature centers of excellence in cardiology, critical care, Gastroenterology, mother & childcare, neonatology and orthopedics. To make quality healthcare accessible and affordable to common man, we are expanding our services to all the districts of Telangana. In addition, we plan to introduce an advanced Emergency Department equipped with a helicopter ambulance, diagnostic facilities, homecare and doctor -on -call. We also plan to double our bed capacity in the near future.

Poised to cater to 1000 care seekers every day at our Hyderabad centers, PRATHIMA’s operational excellence stems from a synergy of process-driven, evidence-based medical practice, patent-first approach, well-trained support staff and the focus on doing the right thing for every patient.

Foundation

Prathima Foundation, a unit of the Prathima Educational Society, is a service-oriented organization that offers a wide range of health care service to the rural population.

The Foundation creates awareness about wellness and hygiene to the rural public by making them understand about their importance and to facilitate them with proper treatment. Towards this, the Foundation frequently conducts various health camps at different places. Also, Prathima Foundation initiated Free Children Heart Surgery camps in association with U.K. Based doctors, Skill Development Centres at Raikal and Nagaram, Vaaradhi Study Centre at Karimnagar, Free Ambulance services in Karimnagar, Health camps and Blood donation camps for thalassemia and Sickle cell patients, Encouraging students who are studying well but need financial support and many more social activities.
